Positions Available: Junior Partner, Senior Associate, Junior Associate, General Member

Junior Partner: A Junior partner's vote is called upon for executive decisions. A junior partner, after some time, can work their way up to a senior partner

Senior Associate: A senior associate will assist in club management. A senior associate can make partner after some time.

Junior Associate: A junior associate will assist a senior associate in club management. They will also be responsible for assisting less-experienced members, and serving as mentors. A junior associate can work their way up to senior associate.

General Member: A general member will assist in various club projects, as well as enhance their computer science and engineering skills. A general member can work their way up to a junior associate.

NOTE - Please note that regardless of your position, your voice will always be welcome at all club meetings and decisions

Also, do note that your application will be subject to review. While all general members are welcome, there is limited room for junior associates and above.
